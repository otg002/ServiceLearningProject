Western Tech Student Survey

The Survey:

The survey entitled "Freshman AP Class Opinions" was taken by Owen Gray (the creator of this website).
It documented the opinions of Western Tech students on AP classes as well as how available AP classes were
to WT students. It revealed that most students received zero AP class options in Freshman year and that those
who did received the AP class option as a requirement as part of their magnet course. It also showed that the
majority of students wish they had more AP course options and feel they would benefit from their GT classes
instead being AP classes. Additionally, the students have been proven ready based of the 100% 4.0 GPAs found
on the survey. The only AP student found their class very enjoyable.
