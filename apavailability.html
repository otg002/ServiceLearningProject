AP Availability

The study on Western Tech students—detailed further on its dedicated page—revealed
that most freshmen at WT had little to no access to AP. This coupled with the benefits
of AP, especially for lower-classmen and 'academically disadvantaged' students shows
that this is a mistake. It is depriving freshmen of rich oportunities for growth and
improvement as well as underestimating them by assuming they cannot handle college-level
classes for little reason aside from age. This as well as the fact that AP classes *used*
to be offered at Western Tech for freshmen (Noah Gray has testified that he had at least
one AP class freshman year) leads to the seemingly obvious point that AP classes should
be made more available to freshmen in the future.
